About Us

TechX-ENIS

TechX is an IEEE CS event designed for students and young professionals. It focuses on new technologies and offers various sessions, including technical demonstrations, talks on emerging technologies, and interactions with industry experts. TechX also emphasizes leadership development and soft skills, providing sessions to help students become effective leaders and prepare for interviews.

IEEE ENIS Studnet Branch

IEEE National Engineering School Of Sfax (ENIS) Student Branch was established on April 1st, 2009. It offers to its members the opportunity to meet and learn from their fellows and the professionals in the field. Our activities offer numerous educational and technical benefits through special projects, activities, meetings, tours and field trips.

IEEE ENIS CS SBC

The IEEE Computer Society has built a strong reputation for its advocacy of computer science and engineering. At the National School of Engineering of Sfax, the Enis Student Branch Chapter was founded on January 27, 2010, with a clear mission: to equip its members with vital Information Technology knowledge, thus enhancing their career prospects and honing their abilities. By organizing enriching programs and fostering valuable networking connections, the chapter empowers ambitious individuals to thrive amidst the constantly changing technology domain.
